What Are AI Agents?

AI agents are autonomous software systems that perceive their environment, make decisions, and take actions to achieve specific goals. Unlike traditional programs that follow rigid instructions, agents can adapt, learn, and operate independently.

From customer service bots to code-writing assistants, AI agents are reshaping every industry. They combine large language models with tool use, memory, and planning capabilities to accomplish complex multi-step tasks.

As someone who runs a business in this space, I get to work at the cutting edge of what's possible with autonomous AI systems every single day.
